Introducing Divitopia

Making crypto easy

Download for Chrome

Auto Currency Conversion

Divitopia supports Cryptopia, Poloniex, Binance, EtherDelta, Etherscan, and more! When you hover over a crypto currency price, Divitopia will display an instant price conversion in the currencies you've selected.

Buy & Sell Alerts

Experience less anxiety when creating buy/sell orders. Divitopia will alert you if the target price is below the asking price threshold, so you don't have to worry about losing your cryptocurrency.

Major Currencies Supported

Divitopia supports many fiat and crypto currencies. Enjoy the flexibility when converting between many currencies. You can also customise the conversion tooltip to display over 26 different fiat prices.

Instant Conversion Anywhere

Need to convert a price on another website or exchange? No problem! Simply select over the price and right click it. You will be able to convert it as a Bitcoin, Bitcoin Cash, Divx, Ethereum or Litecoin currency.
